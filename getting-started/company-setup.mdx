## Set up your company details

After signing up, your company profile holds the name, address, and contact details that appear across NoSavvy. Setting it up is the first step before adding team members or creating jobs.

### Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info" collapsed="false">
  You must be signed in as an **Owner** to edit company settings. Managers and Techs can view their own account settings, but they cannot access or update company settings or client form settings.
</Callout>

### Editing your company profile

Use the company profile page to add your business details and main contact information.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings" icon="settings" title-type="p">
    From the Triage dashboard, open **Settings**. You will see sections for **Company**, **Client-facing**, and **You**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open Company profile" icon="arrow-right" title-type="p">
    In the **Company** section, click **Company profile**. This opens the form where you manage your company details.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Update your business information" icon="edit" title-type="p">
    Enter your **company name**, which is required. Then add your **street address**, **city**, **state**, and **zip code**.

    Select your state from the dropdown list. Your zip code should be 5 digits.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Update your contact information" icon="users" title-type="p">
    Add your **phone number** and **email address**. NoSavvy uses these details in client-facing communications.

    Enter the phone number in the format `(555) 123-4567`.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save your changes" icon="check-circle" title-type="p">
    Click **Save Changes** when you finish editing. A success message appears at the top of the page when the save is complete.

    If you do not want to keep your edits, click **Cancel** to return to Settings.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Configuring client-facing forms

NoSavvy includes three client-facing forms that you can tailor to your workflow: **Pre-Visit Confirmation**, **Crew Checklist**, and **Post-Job Feedback**. New companies start with all standard form fields enabled, so you can adjust them as needed instead of building them from scratch.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Client forms" icon="settings" title-type="p">
    From **Settings**, click **Client forms** in the **Company** section. This opens the form settings page.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Customize the Pre-Visit Confirmation form" icon="edit" title-type="p">
    Turn standard fields on or off for:

    - confirm appointment time
    - access instructions
    - pet information
    - special requests

    Add up to **5 custom questions** for your clients.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Customize the Crew Checklist" icon="check-circle" title-type="p">
    Turn standard tasks on or off for:

    - arrive on time
    - wear uniform
    - greet client
    - perform walkthrough
    - take photos

    Add up to **10 custom tasks** for your team.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Customize the Post-Job Feedback form" icon="edit" title-type="p">
    Turn standard fields on or off for:

    - service rating
    - comments
    - follow-up permission

    Add up to **5 custom questions** for client feedback.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save each form after you update it" icon="save" title-type="p">
    Each form saves independently. Click **Save** after configuring each form so your changes are applied to that form.
  </Step>
</Steps>
