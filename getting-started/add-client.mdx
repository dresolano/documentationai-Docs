## Add your first client

Clients are the businesses you clean for. Each client record holds their contact details, service location, and notes so you have what you need when creating and scheduling jobs.

## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info" collapsed="false">
  Sign in as an **Owner** or **Manager** before you add a client. Techs cannot create or view client records.
</Callout>

## Adding a client

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the Clients page" title-type="p">
    From the Triage dashboard, open the **Clients** page. This page shows all clients for your company and gives you access to client management actions.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Add Client" title-type="p">
    Select **Add Client** to open the client creation form.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter the client's business name" title-type="p">
    Add the client's business name. This name identifies the client throughout NoSavvy, and it is required.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter the primary contact name" title-type="p">
    Add the name of the main person you communicate with at the client's business. This field is required.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add contact details" title-type="p">
    Enter the client's email address and phone number. Both fields are optional, but they help when you need to contact the client quickly.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter the service location" title-type="p">
    Add the street address, city, state, and zip code for the service location. This tells your team where to go for the job.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add any client notes" title-type="p">
    Use the notes field for details such as alarm codes, parking instructions, or other facility information. This field is optional.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Save" title-type="p">
    Select **Save** to create the client. A success message appears, then NoSavvy redirects you to the client's detail page.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Creating a client from the job form

Create a client without leaving the job form when you are creating or editing a job. Next to the client selector, click **+ New Client** to open a quick form with the same fields.

## What's next

<Callout kind="tip" collapsed="false">
  Now that your first client is in NoSavvy, create and schedule your first job so your team can see where to go and what work to complete.
</Callout>
