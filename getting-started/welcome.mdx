## Keep cleaning operations in one place

NoSavvy helps commercial cleaning companies manage jobs, teams, schedules, clients, and field workflows from one platform. It gives owners, managers, and technicians a shared, role-aware workspace for day-to-day operations.

## Who is NoSavvy for?

NoSavvy is built for commercial cleaning companies that manage crews, client sites, and recurring field work. It fits teams that need a clearer way to coordinate work across the office and the field.

You can use NoSavvy to keep schedules current, track job progress, manage client records, and organize team access by company. If your business depends on technicians showing up at the right place with the right job details, NoSavvy is designed for that kind of operation.

## Your role in NoSavvy

Your access in NoSavvy depends on your role. Each role is designed around the work that person needs to do.

| Role        | Access                                                                     |
| ----------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Owner**   | Full company control, including settings, team management, and all records |
| **Manager** | Operational access for jobs, clients, and team coordination                |
| **Tech**    | Field access scoped to assigned jobs and their own work                    |

This role-based structure helps keep daily work focused. It also supports company-level separation, so records stay tied to the correct company.
