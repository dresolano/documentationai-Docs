## Add teammates to your company

Team members are the people who will use NoSavvy alongside you — managers who coordinate jobs and techs who do the field work. Adding them takes a full name, email address, and role.

### Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info">

You must be signed in as an **Owner** to manage team members. Managers and Techs cannot access team management.

</Callout>

### Inviting a team member

<Steps>
<Step title="Go to the Team page" icon="users" title-type="p">

From the Triage dashboard, open the **Team** page. This is the team management page where you can invite, edit, and remove members.

If this is your first invite, the list will usually show only your own account.

</Step>
<Step title="Click Add Team Member" icon="plus" title-type="p">

Click **Add Team Member** to open the invite form.

</Step>
<Step title="Enter their full name and email address" icon="edit" title-type="p">

Enter the person's **Full Name** and **Email Address**. NoSavvy sends the invitation to that email address.

</Step>
<Step title="Choose a role" icon="shield" title-type="p">

Select **Owner**, **Manager**, or **Tech**. The role controls what the person can see and do in NoSavvy.

**Tech** is selected by default.

</Step>
<Step title="Click Send Invitation" icon="check-circle" title-type="p">

Click **Send Invitation** to finish. A success message appears, then NoSavvy redirects you back to the **Team** page.

When the redirect finishes, your team list is visible again.

</Step>
</Steps>

### What happens after you invite someone

After you send the invitation, NoSavvy handles the next step based on whether that email address already belongs to an existing account. If the person already has a NoSavvy account from another company, they get an email saying they have been added to your company and can log in and switch to your company right away.

If the person is new to NoSavvy, NoSavvy creates their account automatically and sends an invitation email with a link to set their password. That password setup link stays valid for 24 hours.

### Managing team members

Use the **Team** page to keep your staff list current as your company grows. The table shows each member's name, email, role, active jobs, and available actions.

- Click **Edit** next to a team member to update their name, phone, bio, or role.
- Click **Remove** to remove someone from your company. They keep their NoSavvy account.
- Check the **Active Jobs** column to see how many active jobs each team member has.
- You cannot remove yourself from the **Team** page.

### Choosing the right role

<Callout kind="tip">

Give **Tech** to field workers who only need to see assigned jobs. Give **Manager** to office staff who coordinate jobs and clients. Reserve **Owner** for people who need full control of company settings and team management.

</Callout>
