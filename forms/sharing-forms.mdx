## Share forms from the job detail page

Share the right form at the right time without leaving the job detail page. Use the Form Links card to copy a link for client communication before or after the job, or to send the crew checklist to your team.

## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info" collapsed="false">

You need access to the job detail page. Any team member assigned to a job can copy form links.

</Callout>

## Copy and share a form link

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the job detail page" title-type="p">

  Open the job you want to work with. The form links appear on that job's detail page.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the Form Links card" title-type="p">

  Look in the main column for the **Form Links** card. The card shows these three forms in order:

  - **Pre-Visit Confirmation**
  - **Crew Checklist**
  - **Post-Job Feedback**

  </Step>
  <Step title="Copy the link you want to share" title-type="p">

  Click **Copy Link** next to the form you want. After you click it, the button shows a checkmark and **Copied!** for 2 seconds.

  That confirms the link is in your clipboard and ready to paste.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Send the link to the right person" title-type="p">

  Paste the link into an email or message.

  - Send **Pre-Visit Confirmation** and **Post-Job Feedback** to your client.
  - Send **Crew Checklist** to your team.

  </Step>
</Steps>

## How links work

<Callout kind="info" collapsed="false">

Each form link uses a signed token for security. Only someone with the exact link can access the form, and no login is required. Links are unique to each job and stay valid for 90 days.

</Callout>

<Callout kind="info" collapsed="false">

The **Crew Checklist** works differently from the other two forms. Team members must be logged in to NoSavvy to use it, and it is not a public link for clients. **Pre-Visit Confirmation** and **Post-Job Feedback** work without a login.

</Callout>

## When to share each form

- **Pre-Visit Confirmation**: Send this to the client a day or two before the appointment so they can confirm the time, share access instructions, and flag pets or special requests.
- **Crew Checklist**: Share this with your team, not the client. Your crew opens it on site to complete standard tasks and any custom tasks you have configured. Completing the checklist moves the job to **In Progress** automatically.
- **Post-Job Feedback**: Send this after the job is marked **Completed** so the client can rate the service and leave comments. Negative ratings automatically flag the job with an issue.
