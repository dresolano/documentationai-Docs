## Manage your team

Build and manage your team from one place. Owners can invite people, assign the right role, update profile details, and remove someone from the company when needed.

<Callout kind="info">

Team management is available to **Owners only**. Owners can open the Team page at `/team/`. Managers and Techs cannot access the Team page.

</Callout>

## The Team page

Use the Team page at `/team/` to see everyone in your company and manage their access. Each row shows one team member and gives you quick actions for updating or removing them.

The table includes five columns:

| Column | What it shows |
| --- | --- |
| **Name** | The team member's full name |
| **Email** | The team member's email address |
| **Role** | A badge showing **Owner**, **Manager**, or **Tech** |
| **Active Jobs** | A colored status dot and a count showing how many active jobs that person has |
| **Actions** | **Edit** and **Remove** actions for that member |

The **Active Jobs** column helps you see workload at a glance. The colored dot shows whether the member currently has active work, and the count shows how many active jobs are assigned to them.

## Inviting a team member

Add new people from the Team page. You choose their role during the invite, and NoSavvy handles the rest.

<Steps>
<Step title="Open the Team page" icon="users" title-type="p">

Go to `/team/` and click **Add Team Member**.

</Step>

<Step title="Enter their name and email" icon="edit" title-type="p">

Enter the person's **full name** and **email address**. Both fields are required.

</Step>

<Step title="Choose a role" icon="shield" title-type="p">

Select one of the role cards. **Tech** is selected by default.

- **Tech** — Field technician. Can view assigned jobs and complete checklists.
- **Manager** — Can create jobs, manage clients, and view reports. Cannot edit company settings.
- **Owner** — Full access to everything, including team management.

</Step>

<Step title="Send the invitation" icon="arrow-right" title-type="p">

Click **Send Invitation**. A success message appears, then you return to the Team page.

</Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="info">

What happens next depends on whether the email already belongs to a NoSavvy account. If the person is new to NoSavvy, an account is created and they receive an invitation email with a link to set their password. That link stays valid for 24 hours.

If the person already has a NoSavvy account through another company, they receive a notification that they have been added to your company. They can sign in and switch to your company right away.

</Callout>

<Callout kind="info">

Owners can assign any role: **Tech**, **Manager**, or **Owner**. Only an Owner can promote someone to **Owner**.

</Callout>

## Editing a team member

Update a team member when their contact details, notes, or responsibilities change. You can also change their role from the edit screen.

<Steps>
<Step title="Open the member record" icon="users" title-type="p">

On the Team page, click **Edit** next to the person you want to update.

</Step>

<Step title="Update their details" icon="edit" title-type="p">

Change any of these fields as needed:

- **Full Name**
- **Phone Number**
- **Bio or Notes**
- **Role**

</Step>

<Step title="Confirm email changes" icon="mail" title-type="p">

If you change the email address, NoSavvy starts a two-step confirmation process.

- A confirmation email goes to the **new** address.
- The confirmation link stays valid for 24 hours.
- A separate notification goes to the **old** address.
- The email does not change until the person confirms it from the new address.

</Step>

<Step title="Save changes" icon="check-circle" title-type="p">

Click **Save Changes** to apply the update.

</Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="info">

If you try to edit your own team member record, NoSavvy sends you to `/my-profile/` instead. Your own email and role are managed through your profile and Owner permissions.

</Callout>

## Removing a member

Remove someone from your company from the Team page. Click **Remove** next to their name, then confirm the dialog to finish the action.

Removing a member only removes their access to your company. The person keeps their NoSavvy account and any memberships they have in other companies.

You cannot remove yourself. The **Remove** button does not appear next to your own name, and self-removal is blocked.

## My Profile

Every user can manage their own profile at `/my-profile/`. This page is for personal details, not team-level permission changes.

Users can:

- Upload an avatar in **JPG** or **PNG** format
- Use an image up to **2 MB**
- Update their **name**
- Update their **phone number**
- Update their **bio or notes**

If no avatar is uploaded, NoSavvy shows the person's initials instead.

Email and role are read-only on **My Profile**. An Owner must change a team member's role, and email changes are controlled from the team management flow.
