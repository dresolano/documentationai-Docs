<Update label="July 19, 2026" description="Version 1.3.0">
  **Features**

  - Commercial and residential account types for clients — tag clients as commercial or residential with dynamic form fields, badges, and list filters. See [Adding a Client](/clients/add-client) and [Editing Client Information](/clients/edit-client).
  - Flag Issue button on job detail pages — flag problems with optional context notes; owners and managers are notified immediately. See [Viewing Job Details](/jobs/job-details).
  - Demo workspace for platform admins — a pre-seeded sample workspace for training, demos, and screenshots, with a one-click reset tool.
  - Interactive company switcher in the sidebar — switch between companies instantly without a full page reload.
  - Account type filter on the reports dashboard — scope revenue and performance charts by commercial or residential clients. See [Reports Dashboard](/reports/dashboard).

  **Improvements**

  - All transactional emails (invitations, notifications, issue alerts) now use the branded HTML template with proper sender addresses.
  - Pre-visit and feedback forms automatically adjust their wording based on whether the client is commercial or residential. See [Pre-Visit Confirmation Forms](/forms/pre-visit-confirmation) and [Post-Job Feedback Forms](/forms/post-job-feedback).
  - Updated terminology throughout the app — "customers" is now "clients" everywhere, with copy tailored to cleaning businesses.
  - Password reset flow improved — cleaner redirect, branded email, and a stronger 10-character minimum.
  - Unified role badges (Owner / Manager / Tech) across the sidebar, team list, and profile pages. See [User Roles and Permissions](/getting-started/roles-permissions).
  - Faster team management page for companies with many staff members. See [Managing Team Members](/team/manage-team).
  - Redesigned login, signup, and password reset pages.
  - Signup rate limiting added to prevent automated abuse.

  **Fixes**

  - Fixed short-duration jobs clipping their title and time text on the schedule board. See [Schedule Board](/jobs/schedule-board).
  - Fixed company names showing HTML entities (e.g. `&#8217;`) in email subject lines.
  - Fixed the home page greeting sometimes showing an email prefix instead of the user's first name.
  - Fixed the jobs list showing a blank error when no company is selected — it now redirects to the home page.
</Update>

<Update label="July 14, 2026" description="Version 1.2.0">
  **Features**

  - Platform admin home page with an instructional card when no tenant is selected — explains how to use the Demo workspace and company switcher.

  **Improvements**

  - Platform admins now land in the Demo workspace by default on login instead of an arbitrary tenant.
  - Email sender addresses now use the site's own domain for better deliverability.
  - Public form submissions are validated before being stored, preventing incomplete or invalid entries.
  - New signups load directly into the company workspace without an extra redirect.

  **Fixes**

  - Fixed platform admins getting stuck in a support view for a company they're no longer a member of.
</Update>

<Update label="July 12, 2026" description="Version 1.1.0">
  **Features**

  - Sidebar navigation with links to Home, Schedule, Jobs, Clients, Team, Reports, and Settings.
  - Job creation, editing, and detail pages with full status lifecycle (Scheduled → Ready → In Progress → Completed → Archived), line items, and recurring schedule options. See [How to Create a Job](/jobs/create-job), [How to Edit or Update a Job](/jobs/edit-job), and [Viewing Job Details](/jobs/job-details).
  - Drag-and-drop schedule board with per-tech columns, unassigned job queue, and mobile-friendly list view. See [Using the Schedule Board](/jobs/schedule-board) and [Drag-and-Drop Scheduling](/jobs/drag-drop-scheduling).
  - Client management — searchable list, add/edit pages with address fields, detail page with full job history, and inline quick-add modal on job forms. See [How to Add a Client](/clients/add-client) and [Viewing Client History](/clients/client-history).
  - Reports dashboard with KPI cards, trend charts, and date range selector. See [Understanding the Reports Dashboard](/reports/dashboard).
  - Redesigned home page with role-based views — stat cards and quick actions for managers, "Next Up" job card for techs.
  - Team management pages with role selector cards and active job count indicators. See [Adding and Managing Team Members](/team/manage-team).
  - Platform admin support view — enter a tenant's workspace with a visible banner and exit button.
  - Completion guard modal — warns of incomplete pre-visit or checklist before marking a job complete.

  **Improvements**

  - Public forms (pre-visit confirmation, crew checklist, post-job feedback) now validate signed access tokens before accepting submissions. See [Pre-Visit Confirmation Forms](/forms/pre-visit-confirmation) and [Post-Job Feedback Forms](/forms/post-job-feedback).
</Update>
